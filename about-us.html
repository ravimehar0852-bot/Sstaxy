About Us

Built for people who've had a bad cab ride before

SS TAXY started with a simple complaint: fares that changed after the trip, drivers who didn't know the route, and cars that showed up looking nothing like the photo. So we built a taxi service around three non-negotiables — the fare quoted is the fare charged, the driver is verified before they're on our roster, and someone real answers the phone.

Today SS TAXY runs outstation, local, and airport transfers out of Jaipur, covering the major Rajasthan circuits — Udaipur, Jodhpur, Ajmer and Pushkar, Kota — along with regular runs to Delhi and Agra. Whether it's a solo work trip, a family wedding convoy, or a same-day airport dash, the same standard applies to every booking.

How we vet a driver

Every driver on the SS TAXY roster holds a valid commercial license, passes a background check, and completes a route-familiarity review before their first outstation booking. We re-verify documentation on a rolling basis rather than once at onboarding.

How we price a trip

Rates are set per kilometre by vehicle class, with toll, interstate permit, and night driver allowance itemised separately and shown before you confirm. There's no destination-based surge — a Tuesday afternoon booking and a Friday evening one for the same route cost the same base fare.

What's next

We're expanding our verified fleet across more Rajasthan cities and building out corporate account options for teams that need recurring airport and inter-city transfers. If there's a route you need that isn't listed yet, tell our dispatch desk — we're actively adding new corridors.
