Cancelling a booking

Cancellations made at least 2 hours before the scheduled pickup are free of charge. Cancellations inside that 2-hour window may carry a partial charge to cover the driver's dispatch cost.

No-shows

If a driver waits at the pickup point for more than 10 minutes past the scheduled time without contact from the rider, the booking may be marked a no-show and the dispatch charge applied.

Refunds

Where an advance payment was made and the trip is cancelled within the free-cancellation window, the refund is processed to the original payment method within 5–7 business days.

Service issues

If a trip doesn't meet the standard we've committed to — a driver significantly late without notice, a mismatched vehicle, or a billing discrepancy — contact our dispatch desk within 24 hours of the trip so we can review and resolve it.
